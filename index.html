Delicious Menu

  • Butter Chicken

    All Time Best ₹ 2,200

    Succulent pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, simmered in a rich tomato-based gravy with butter and cream, resulting in a velvety texture and a harmonious blend of sweet and savory flavors.

  • Biryani

    ₹ 800

    Fragrant long-grain rice layered with tender pieces of marinated meat (chicken, lamb, or goat), infused with aromatic spices, herbs, and saffron, creating a dish that is both flavorful and aromatic.

  • Masala Dosa

    ₹ 400

    Crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a spicy potato masala, served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar, offering a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, and a staple breakfast item in South India.

  • Rogan Josh

    New ₹ 2,100

    Tender chunks of meat (usually lamb or goat) cooked in a flavorful gravy enriched with Kashmiri spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, resulting in a dish that is rich, aromatic, and deeply satisfying.

  • Paneer Tikka

    ₹ 700

    Cubes of paneer cheese marinated in a tangy and spicy yogurt-based marinade, skewered and grilled until golden and charred, offering a smoky flavor and a burst of spices in every bite.

  • Pav Bhaji

    ₹ 300

    A delightful combination of spicy vegetable mash (bhaji) made from potatoes, tomatoes, onions, and peas, served with buttered bread rolls (pav), offering a burst of flavors and textures.
